## Why shorturl.fit?

Managing links shouldn't be complicated or expensive. **[shorturl.fit](https://www.shorturl.fit/)** was built to give marketers, content creators, and small businesses a simple, no-cost way to shorten URLs, customize them with their own branding, and track how those links perform — without needing a subscription or complex setup.

Whether you're sharing links on social media, embedding them in emails, or printing them on flyers, having a clean, branded URL makes a real difference in click-through rates and trust.

## Key Features

###  Custom Branded URL Shortener
Turn long, messy URLs into clean, memorable short links. With our **[Branded URL Shortener](https://www.shorturl.fit/custom_url_shortner/)**, you can create custom aliases that reflect your brand name, campaign, or message.

**Why branded links matter:**
- They build trust — people are more likely to click a link that looks intentional, not random
- They're more shareable on platforms like Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and SMS where space is limited
- They look professional in presentations, business cards, and printed materials

###  QR Code Generator
Need a QR code for your business card, restaurant menu, event flyer, or product packaging? Our **[QR Code Generator](https://www.shorturl.fit/qr_code_generator/)** lets you create scannable QR codes instantly — linked to any URL you choose.

**Common QR code use cases:**
- Restaurant menus and table ordering
- Event registration and ticketing
- Business card contact sharing
- Product packaging and unboxing experiences
- Storefront promotions and window displays

## Getting Started

Using shorturl.fit takes about 10 seconds:

1. Visit **[shorturl.fit](https://www.shorturl.fit/)**
2. Paste your long URL
3. (Optional) Customize your short link alias via the **[Custom URL Shortener](https://www.shorturl.fit/custom_url_shortner/)**
4. Click "Shorten" — done!

For QR codes, head to the **[QR Code Generator](https://www.shorturl.fit/qr_code_generator/)**, enter your URL, and download your code in seconds.
